Chicken Curry

Description

Being immensly popular in the Indian subcontinent, this dish consists of chicken stewed in an onion and tomato based sauce. It also contains ginger, garlic, and cilantro which serve to add a level of fresh flavor and color. So if you like chicken, with a rich aroma and an unforgettable savory taste, then this recipe is right for you!

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tablespoons curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated fresh ginger root
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • Salt
  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves-cur into bite size pieces
  • 1 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 3/4 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 lemon, huiced
  • 1/2 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Saute onion until lightly browned.
  3. Stir garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar and salt. Continue to stir for two minutes.
  4. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk.
  5. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer for 20 to 25 minutes.
  6. remove bay leaf, and stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper. Simmer 5 more minutes.
