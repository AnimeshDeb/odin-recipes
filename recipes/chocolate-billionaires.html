Chocolate Billionaires

Description

Chocolate Billionaires are baked goods that contain chocolate, caramel, and Rice Krispies. This means that if you like crunchy, chocolatey goodness, then this recipe is for you!

Ingredients

  • 1 package (14 ouncs) of caramels
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1-1/2 cups of chopped pecans
  • 1 cup Rice Krispies
  • 3 cups milk chocolate chips
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons shortening

Steps

  1. Get two baking sheets and line them with waxed paper.
  2. Grease the paper and set it aside.
  3. Retrieve a large and heave saucepan and place mixed caramels and water in it.
  4. Cook and stir over low heat until the contents become smooth.
  5. Stir in pecans and cereal until it becomes coated.
  6. Drop teaspoonfuls of the contents onto prepared pans.
  7. Refrigerate for 10 minutes or until firm.
  8. Meanwhile, meltt chocolate chips and shortening in a microwave.
  9. Stir the contents until smooth.
  10. Dip the candy into chocolate, coating all sides, and allow excess to drip off.
  11. Place the contents on prepared pans and refrigerate until set.
