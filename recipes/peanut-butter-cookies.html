Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

Peanut butter cookies are one of the common and favored baked good found in many parts of the world. It is made from peanut butter and chocolate kisses. If you have a sweet tooth or just need to eat something that will make you smile, then this recipe is for you.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 30 milk chocolate kisses

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Crea peanut butter and sugar until they are light and fluffy. Beat the contents in egg and vanilla.
  2. Roll the contents into 1-1/4 inch balls.
  3. Place them 2 inch apart on ungreased baking sheets.
  4. Bake until the tops are slightly cracked for 10-12 minutes
  5. Place 1 chocolate kises into the center of each cookie.
  6. Cool them for 5 minutes before removing them from pans into wire racks.
